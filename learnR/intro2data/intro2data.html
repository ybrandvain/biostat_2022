These data are not tidy! Each observation is a quarter of a year, and the variables are – year, quarter, and gas let’s tidy theme with the pivot_longer function.

pivot_longer(data = ukgas, 
             cols = c("quarter_1", "quarter_2", "quarter_3", "quarter_4"),
             names_to = "quarter", 
             names_prefix = "quarter_",
             values_to = "gas") %>%
  head(n = 6)
